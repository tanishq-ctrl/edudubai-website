## General Guidelines for All Images

### Composition Rules:
1. **Subject Position**: Right 50-60% of frame
2. **Subject Direction**: Looking left or toward camera
3. **Background Left**: Simple, darker, gradient-friendly (left 40-50%)
4. **Background Right**: Can be detailed (office, windows, etc.)

### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: Navy blue (#1e3a5f)
- **Accent**: Gold (#d4af37)
- **Neutrals**: White, light gray, charcoal
- **Avoid**: Bright reds, greens, or competing colors

### Lighting:
- Bright, professional lighting
- Natural window light preferred
- Well-lit subject face
- Avoid harsh shadows

### Style:
- Professional corporate photography
- Modern, clean aesthetic
- Photorealistic (not illustrated)
- High quality, sharp focus

### Subject Characteristics:
- Professional business attire
- Confident, approachable expression
- Diverse representation (mix of genders, ethnicities)
- Age range: 25-45 years
- Well-groomed, professional appearance

## Tools to Use

### Recommended AI Image Generators:
1. **Midjourney** (Best quality, most realistic)
2. **DALL-E 3** (Good for specific compositions)
3. **Stable Diffusion XL** (Free, customizable)
4. **Adobe Firefly** (Good for commercial use)

### Midjourney Tips:
Add these parameters to your prompts:
```
--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6 --q 2
```
