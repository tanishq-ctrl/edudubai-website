## Whisk-Specific Tips:

### Using These Backgrounds with Whisk:

1. **Generate the Background:**
   - Use the prompts above to create clean backgrounds
   - Ensure "no people, no text, no logos" is emphasized

2. **Add Your References in Whisk:**
   - Upload your Edu-Dubai logo as a reference
   - Upload certification badges (CAMS, GCI, etc.) as references
   - Whisk will intelligently blend them into the background

3. **Composition Tips:**
   - Backgrounds have "empty center space" for your badge placement
   - Lighting is designed to accommodate overlays
   - Colors complement your navy/gold branding

### Recommended Whisk Settings:

- **Subject:** Your certification badge + Edu-Dubai logo
- **Scene:** Use these background prompts
- **Style:** "Professional corporate photography, premium quality"
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9

### Color Palette Reference:
- **Primary Navy:** #1e3a5f
- **Brand Gold:** #d4af37
- **Accent Colors:** Warm neutrals, subtle blues

### Post-Generation in Whisk:
- Position badges in center or center-left
- Place "Edu-Dubai" logo in top-right corner
- Ensure text is readable against background
- Adjust opacity/blend modes if needed
